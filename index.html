    PINKATHON

    Pinkathon is a running marathon where hundreds of women come together to learn and spread awareness regarding breast cancer ,it is being held at 7 am so make sure you don’t have a very heavy breakfast before participating! :P
    The distance to be covered in the marathon is 4km within RNSIT campus.
    Although running a marathon can be a scary and painful endeavor, training for and running a race can offer a number of benefits for the body, mind, and soul.

    WALATHON

    The costs associated with radiation, chemotherapy, and/or reconstructive surgery can be exorbitant, and so this walkathon is a step towards prevention of breast cancer. Exercise, agrees doctors, is must to keep cancer at bay, along with a healthy diet. Therefore we have organized this walkathon which is an opportunity knocking at your door to educate yourself and those around you too.
